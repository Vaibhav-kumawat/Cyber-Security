Cyber Security

What is Cyber Security?

Cyber security refers to the body of technologies, processes, and practices designed to protect networks, devices, programs, and data from attack, damage, or unauthorized access. Cyber security may also be referred to as information technology security.

Who invented cyber security?

Researcher Bob Thomas

Cyber security began in the 1970s when researcher Bob Thomas created a computer programme called Creeper that could move across ARPANET's network, leaving a breadcrumb trail wherever it went.
